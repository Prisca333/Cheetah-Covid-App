What’s next after covid ?

Will life return to normal or should we be worried?

since the outbreak of Covid-18 across the world the ‘manual’ workforce as we know it has now become revolutionised. with at least 85% of our workforce being forced to adapt and complete work online to minimise unnecessary travel and the transmission of COVID-19

So what does this mean for us?

this has led to a sharp rise in demand for a more ‘hybrid’ type of work force which would consist of a blend between face to face and working from home. As well as the possible of ‘hybrid work’. Covid has also introduced wearing face masks in public as the new ‘norm’.
